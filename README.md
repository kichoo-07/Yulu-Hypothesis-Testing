## 📌 Project Overview
Yulu is India’s leading micro-mobility service provider, offering unique vehicles for the daily commute. This project utilizes statistical Hypothesis Testing to determine the significant factors that influence the demand for shared electric cycles in the Indian market.

## 🎯 Business Problem
To maximize revenue and ensure vehicle availability, Yulu needs to anticipate demand fluctuations. The objective of this analysis is to statistically validate whether factors like weather conditions, weekdays, and seasons have a significant impact on bike rentals, enabling data-driven inventory distribution.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Analytical Tools
* **Language:** Python
* **Libraries:** Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, SciPy, Statsmodels
* **Statistical Concepts:** 2-Sample T-Tests, ANOVA, Chi-Square Tests, p-value evaluation, Data Distribution Analysis

## 📊 Key Insights & Business Value
* **Working Days vs. Weekends (T-Test):** Conducted a 2-sample T-test to determine if there is a statistically significant difference in demand between working days and non-working days.
* **Weather & Seasonality (ANOVA):** Applied Analysis of Variance (ANOVA) to prove that weather conditions (Clear, Misty, Rain/Snow) and seasonal changes drastically affect user behavior and ride volumes.
* **Data-Backed Recommendations:** Formulated strategies for dynamic pricing during peak seasons and optimized bike placement near transit hubs during adverse weather conditions.
